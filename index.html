Oddventure is a jRPG about the missadventures of Charlie - the nihilistic and rebelious teenage girl with anger issues and social awkwardness who searches for her troublesome brother Bonzo and ultimately for a way back home.
Fight your inner demons and choose between either starring into the abyss or reaching for the spark of hope. The cozyness and light-hearted stuff meets the dark humor, oddness and crazyness. And… Perverts. br Who are not perverts, but they kinda are.

SCREENSHOTS

You end up in Kingdom of Luxia, a land straight from original Grimm Brothers’ fairy tales with some dark, nietzschean twist. Throughout the game you will meet characters who will either want to help or do you a harm. Player is on bizzare journey full of cursed-image-like enemies, good guys, bad guys, talking frogs, perverts and the versions of fairytalish creatures which are more of god’s abomination.

Bonzo

Overenthusiastic and playful little fellow. Always on sugar rush and ready to jump into action. He fights with speed with suspicious cross looking sword in hand and laugh on his mouth. Some say he is Charlie’s younger brother.

Charlie

A nihilistic, rebellious and socially awkward teenage girl with anger issues, who likes to wear oversized clothes. Oddventure’s main heroine, on a quest to find a way back home. Being a powerful MOODMANCER and “sharpthrower”, she’s the leader of the party.

Dodo and Mr. Rabbit

Two klutzes, forced by Jack Grimm, into helping Charlie and Bonzo during their journey. What they lack in “smarts” and “bravery” departments they make up with being loyal. They’re always ready to protect their friends. Also, they’re tea enthusiasts.

Forestia

Noble knight born into the most prominent family in whole Luxia kingdom. Her wrath and rage is well known in border regions. She treats witch and dragon hunting as hobby.

Liselotte

Usually called by simpletons a Most Intelligent Lady of the Forest. A maleficent and powerful hedge witch , but when in bed she dreams of very own spa center…

Priest

In which some see the madness others see the calling. But no one ever try to persuade him, as he is also an owner of F**** grizzly bear !!!
