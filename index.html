Oddventure is an upcoming RPG about fighting your inner demons. Play as Chralie, a troubled teenage girl who searches for her brother and, ultimately, a way back home. Make powerful allies or enemies on a bizzare journey of discovering yourself and the truth behind what has really happened..

Name: Charlie

Occupation: Moodmancer

Charlie is the main protagonist of the game. A nihilistic, rebellious and socially awkward teenage girl with anger issues, who likes to wear oversized clothes. She has a special gift that can change the reality around her. Either for better or worse. Trying to find her brother and ultimately a way back home, Charlie gets entangled in the comedy of errors.

Name: Mr. Rabbit and Dodo

Occupation: Anti-bards

Jack Grimm’s loyal servats.
Who are real animals and totally not some costumed humans. Mr. Rabbit is a gluttonous slowpoke. Dodo is a neurotic scaredy cat. They like throwing tea parties at random places at any time. They cannot exist one without each other. Goofy sidekicks, who love to play instruments and sing. But they’re awful at it.

Name: Jack Grimm

Occupation: Mad Hatter

A mysterious man living a lonely life in the middle of nowhere.
He’s the only known character
that ended up in this fairytalish world before Charlie and Bonzo. Unlike her, he decided to stay here as encountering the magical creatures was his long lasting dream. Not much is known about his past. He’s always ready to give a good advice. He holds some secrets yet to be discovered.

Name: Bonzo

Occupation: Hero

Charlie’s troublesome brother who's always ready to jump into the action. Overenthusiastic and playful little fellow. A true hero everyone needs, but not the one everyone deserves. Even though he doesn’t really understand the general cocnept of morality.
