About:

Oddventure is a lore-rich turn-based crazy RPG set in a cursed fairytale world. Fight your inner demons and reach for the spark of hope, or stare directly into the abyss.


Gameplay:

Battle Enemies – Classic turn-based combat with a twist: use the MOOD of your characters! Every battle can be ended in different ways. Be the good guy. Or the bad guy. Or even worse...




Explore and Interact – Meet new people (or creatures). Forge friendships or make enemies. In this world, every character can be talked to and has something to say, whether it be something important to the story or a bad joke. Don't forget to check the objects around you, either. Even they may have something interesting to say.




Make Choices – Every decision matters. It’s up to you how the story plays out and which of several endings you recieve.




Solve Puzzles - Between battles, explore a world filled with cute and weird puzzles full of odd creatures.


Story:

Oddventure is a JRPG about the misadventures of Charlie - a nihilistic and rebelious teenage girl with anger issues and social awkwardness. Charlie searches for her troublesome brother Bonzo and ultimately for a way back home.




The setting is the Kingdom of Luxia, a land straight from original Grimm Brothers’ fairy tales with a dark, Nietzschean twist.




Oddventure is a bizzare journey full of cursed image-like enemies, good guys, bad guys, talking frogs, perverts, and horrifying fairy tale creatures corrupted by darkness.




Fight your inner demons. Choose whether to stare into the abyss or reach for the spark of hope. Cozy storytelling and light-hearted adventure meets dark humor, craziness, and oddness. And perverts. (They're not really that perverted. Or maybe they are.)
